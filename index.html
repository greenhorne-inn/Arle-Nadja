Who is Arle Nadja?

Arle is the main protagonist of the RPG series Madou Monogatari (Sorcery Saga), and the first protagonist of its eclipsingly-popular puzzle game spinoff, Puyo Puyo. She is a teenage sorceress from the Madou World who loves curry, the color blue, and her loyal companion, Carbuncle. Across both series, she has been on many adventures from as young as 4 years old, to usually 16, and made many friends from various universes.

You can read more about Arle on her Puyo Nexus Wiki page.
